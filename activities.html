DETECTIVE SHEET

"The Detective Sheet" is an educational resource designed for teaching about the context of plants, focusing on "foraging sites" and "foraging landscapes."

Before you begin:
ᨒ Make sure to have your teacher print out the PDF or download it to your mobile device.
ᨒ Remember to bring a pencil and eraser if you're working on paper.
ᨒ Keep the website, handy for reference, while completing the task.
ᨒ Your teacher will allocate a specific amount of time for you to complete the assignment.
ᨒ Always remember to ask your teachers for guidance before foraging anything or if you're unsure about anything.

COOK THE INGREDIENT

"Cook the Ingredient" is an educational resource aimed at enhancing students' understanding of cooking ingredients and culinary techniques. This activity encourages students to explore the unique characteristics of ingredients and their role in cooking.

Before you begin:
ᨒ Remember to wash hands thoroughly.
ᨒ Always follow safety precautions when cooking.
ᨒ Read and understand the recipe carefully before you begin. Pay attention to ingredients, measurements, and cooking techniques.
ᨒ Make sure you know how to use the right equipment.
ᨒ Don't hesitate to ask your teachers for guidance if you're unsure about anything.
